We offer online consultations if you are overseas or if your schedule does not allow for a campus visit. However, if you and your family are in Singapore, you can take the opportunity to visit our campus for a physical tour. An On-site Tour is a great way to experience the Australian International School campus.

To book an online consultation complete the form below and a member of our Admissions Team will be in touch to arrange an appointment at your convenience.
